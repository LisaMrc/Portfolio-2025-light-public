Snow Bunny

During my 3rd semester at the IMAC engineering school, we had to create a 4-second animation with the only constraint being an object that moved from left to right. I chose to represent a winter scene by modeling a rabbit jumping in the snow. Everything was created from A to Z: the rabbit, the trees, and the snow. I was able to deepen my knowledge in all areas, particularly in sculpture, lighting, and rigging.
