Kakudai

Kakudai is the project of the second semester of the second year of MMI. Throughout the semester, we had to create a territory conquest game with no imposed theme (we only had the rules). We chose the theme of Japan in the Edo era, then created everything from positioning to graphic design to the application that allows you to play the game. Then, we set up the stand to be able to present it at our university's board game festival.

Type : School Project
Created : Second year of MMI
Teamwork : Yes, within a group of 8

Softwares Used

Adobe Photoshop (mockups) Adobe InDesign (booklet of the game's rules)
Adobe Illustrator (card and board designs)
Adobe After Effects (app intro, events on the app)
Flutterflow (app)
