Creation of an album

The goal was to create the whole cover and the inside booklet for a music album. I chose "Bounce into the Music" from the band SIAMES. After a detailed analysis of the songs, what they meant, and how the album was constructed, I decided to create a branding that matched with their animation studio, RUDO. We also had to create a few photographs that matched a song and insert them into the booklet. I'm very proud of that work !
